# Chia Seed Pie

## Accessories

- Baking dish: 22 cm.
- Oven

## Ingredients:
- 2 eggs
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 10 grams of vanilla sugar (2 teaspoons)
- 100 grams of sugar (1/2 cup)
- 90 ml vegetable oil
- 65 ml milk
- 100 grams of semolina (2/3 cup)
- 45 grams of chia seeds (3 tablespoons)
- 100 grams of wheat flour (1/2 cup)
- 1 teaspoon baking powder


## Steps

1. Mix eggs, salt, vanilla sugar and sugar.
2. Add oil, milk and mix, 
3. Add flower, baking powder, samolina and chia seeds/
4. Pour the mix to the backing dish
5. Bake in preheated 170Â°C oven for 25-30 minutes.
